Blood 'n Meat

Raw. Bloody. Survival intensified.

About the Mod

Blood 'n Meat (sometimes referred to as Blood 'n Flesh) is a hybrid semi-technology/magic mod for Minecraft that transforms the way you interact with life, death, and power. This mod dives into the essence of living and undead creatures, letting players harvest Blood Droplets from both — each leading to unique paths of progression and corruption.

Your choices determine your fate: become one with the living flesh, embrace the undead blood, or master both. Progress through the dark alchemy of blood and flesh to unlock the techno-magic realm — a twisted world of biomechanical horrors, rivers of blood, and living machines.

Key Features

  • Dual Progression: Follow the Living Flesh or Undead Flesh path — or merge both for ultimate mastery.
  • Bloodflow Energy System: A unique hybrid of organic and mechanical power that fuels machines, rituals, and abilities.
  • Corruption Mechanics: Transform yourself or your world over time as you use blood and flesh power — at a cost.
  • Techno-Magic Crafting: Combine blood, organs, and metal to create new weapons, armors, and machines.
  • Horrific Dimension: Enter the Bloodrealm — a pulsating world of flesh, rivers of blood, and techno-flesh monsters.
  • Advanced Cooking: Cook anything from simple stews to magical-infused meats and brain-enhancing meals.
  • Steampunk Abilities: Channel blood-infused intelligence to access advanced tools and powers.

Progression Overview

  1. Survival & Extraction: Harvest blood from living and undead creatures using primitive tools.
  2. Flesh Processing: Build early devices such as the Coagulator and Blood Extractor.
  3. Blood Tech Expansion: Establish a Bloodflow Network and automate your bio-factory.
  4. Corruption Evolution: Unlock corruption-infused armor, organs, and powers.
  5. Dimensional Travel: Access the Bloodrealm and harness rare corrupted materials.
  6. Ascension: Awaken your inner hemomancer and fuse blood, flesh, and machine into one.

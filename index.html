Blood 'n Meat

A Minecraft mod about flesh, meat, and the mysteries of blood magic.

About the Mod

Blood 'n Meat adds new food items, raw and cooked meats, coagulated blood, and a fresh layer of biological crafting to Minecraft’s world. Still in active development!
